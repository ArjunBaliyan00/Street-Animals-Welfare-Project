JEEV WELFARE SOCIETY

Collaboration With Organization

Jeev Welfare is a compassionate organization dedicated to the welfare of street animals. Located in Jalandhar,
this organization works tirelessly to rescue, rehabilitate, and provide shelter to stray animals, ensuring they receive
the care and attention they deserve. Jeev Welfare focuses on providing medical assistance, food, and a safe
environment for abandoned and injured animals, while also working to raise awareness about animal rights
and responsible pet ownership within the community. Their commitment to creating a better life for street
animals makes them an invaluable resource in the region.
